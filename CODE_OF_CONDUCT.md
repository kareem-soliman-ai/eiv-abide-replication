# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and respectful research community, we
commit to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Focus criticism on ideas and methods, not people.
- Share replication findings honestly and transparently.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting language, or deliberate intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting

To report conduct concerns, contact: kareem.soliman@outlook.com.au.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki
edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of
Conduct.
